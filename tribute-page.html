Neri Oxman

The Founder of Material Ecology

  • 2006 - Launched an interdisciplinary research project at MIT called material ecology, to experiment with generative design.
  • 2010 - Became a professor at MIT, founding the interdisciplinary Mediated Matter group at the MIT Media Lab.
  • 2015 - TED talk on material ecology has been viewed over 2.5 million times.
  • 2016 - Helped launch the open Journal of Design Science, an "antidisciplinary" journal which journal co-founder Joi Ito described as "working in spaces that simply do not fit into any existing academic discipline."
  • 2016 - Collaborated with Bjork creating a mask of the singer's face.
  • 2016 - Collaborated with Iris van Herpen to 3D-print a collection of wearable couture.
  • 2017 - Awarded Tenure at MIT.
  • 2019 - Netflix docu-series Abstract: The Art of Design featured her work in its second season.

"I believe in the balance between dreaming and building."

- Neri Oxman
